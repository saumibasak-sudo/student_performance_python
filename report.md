# Student Performance Analysis Report

## Objective
To analyze factors affecting student exam performance.

## Key Findings
- Female students scored higher on average than male students.
- Increased study hours lead to better exam scores.
- Low attendance negatively affects performance.

## Visual Analysis
- Bar chart compares average scores by gender.
- Line chart shows study hours vs exam score trend.

## Conclusion
Consistent study habits and attendance significantly improve student performance.
